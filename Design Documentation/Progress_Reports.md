Week 2 (April 5 - 11, 2026)

This week I got around detailing the groundwork for a combat system. It's pretty similar to a Dark Souls style. You have your stat blocks which affect the speed of movement, as well as damage dealt, critical hits, and potentially other factors unrelated to combat (such as finding treasure in the overworld). The combat itself would be more dependent on what class and role you play, functioning as a DPS, healer, or tank, but boils down to learning how enemies move and manipulating that for openings where you can attack, heal, etc. I did quickly realize how dependent the system is on a stat system, which I also designed. I'm debating if I want to do random growths (ie Fire Emblem), set growths (most JRPGs), or custom growths (soulsborn). I know how the stats work though. There is **Strength**, which affects physical attack power, C**onstitution** that affects HP and physical defense, **Wit** that affects damage of non-physical attacks, **Will** which affects non-physical defense, **Speed** that affects movement and dodge speed, **Skill** that affects attack damage as well as critical chance and damage, and **Luck** which affects crit chance and damage, as well as drops and loot.
